Supplier accounts & analytics

Track what you owe each vendor. Drug quantities and batches are added under Add pharmacy stock (that flow posts value here when a supplier and cost are set). On each supplier’s ledger you can record manual payables (invoices not from stock) and record payments when you pay the vendor.

Add pharmacy stock (qty, batch, supplier) Stock monitoring Dashboard
Where do I add items & quantities? Use Add pharmacy stock for inventory (drug, quantity, batch, expiry, supplier, cost). That creates the stock batch and increases this supplier’s balance when cost > 0. This page is the money ledger, not the stock form.
