Consumption counts units recorded when medications are dispensed to patients (history log), when walk-in sales are marked dispensed, and when pharmacy staff record stock losses (breakage, expiry, etc.). It is a usage view, not sales revenue.

Avg / day divides total out by the reporting length: {{ effective_window_days }} day(s) for averages and cover math in this view{% if period == 'calendar_month' %} (local calendar month to date){% else %} (rolling window){% endif %}.

Cover divides total on-hand (all batches for that drug) by average daily out. It is indicative only — seasonal spikes or stock-outs distort the average.

Suggest is a heuristic restock line (reorder point plus roughly four weeks at the observed average, capped). Always sanity-check before ordering.
